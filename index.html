About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Full-Stack Developer with over six years of professional experience, specializing in backend Python and frontend JavaScript technologies. I design and build scalable, cloud-based systems using containerization, modern database tools and CI/CD pipelines. My work spans multiple industries - from healthcare and pharmaceutical AI to mobile gaming, banking and product development.

Currently I work as a contract Full-Stack Developer at Holisticon Connect, where I build software solutions for a global medical company - developing Django-based backend services and React frontends with schema-driven UIs for data-intensive pharmaceutical workflows. I also presented on data validation for GenAI in the pharma sector at the AI & Life Sciences Meetup in Stockholm.

In parallel, I collaborate with Fun Crafters, a game development studio in Kraków, where I design interactive web applications and AI-powered tools for the gaming industry - building modular UIs, real-time features and optimized data-driven experiences for mid-core RPGs.

I hold a BSc in Computer Science from University College Dublin and am currently pursuing a Master's in Management at Kozminski University. I've published research on AI incident management at the IEEE Cyber Research Conference and contributed technical articles to IT Professional newspaper.
