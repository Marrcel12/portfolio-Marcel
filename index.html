My ongoing research focuses on enhancing AI incident data sharing for cybersecurity, aligning closely with my aspiration to pursue a part-time PhD in AI governance and data protection. Driven by curiosity and a commitment to innovation, I am dedicated to making significant contributions to the field of computer science.
